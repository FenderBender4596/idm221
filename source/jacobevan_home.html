What Is Jacob Evan?

Jacob Evan is a stripped down, and emotionally intense indie rock project; very different in nature from my work with Dottie Rebel. My first EP, recorded with Quartz Collective Studios, has a heavy focus on acoustic instruments, as well as some electronic elements, and some electric guitars for ambiance.
